How LUNA Assist Works

LUNA Assist is an AI-powered virtual assistant designed to enhance user experience with advanced voice interaction, automation, and intelligence.

Key Features:

  • Voice-activated commands with a simple click on the mic icon.
  • Smart processing to understand and execute user instructions.
  • Integration with various applications and services.
  • Real-time analysis of environment and user reactions.

How to Use:

  1. Click the microphone icon to activate LUNA Assist.
  2. Speak your command clearly, such as "Open Spotify" or "Check Weather".
  3. LUNA Assist processes your request and executes the necessary action.

Enjoy a seamless and interactive experience with LUNA Assist, making tasks easier and more efficient.
